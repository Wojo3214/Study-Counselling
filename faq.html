Degree programmes at Business Academy Aarhus:

At Business Academy Aarhus we have three types of degree programmes taught in English - Academy Profession degrees (AP), Bachelor's degree programmes and Bachelor's top-up degree programmes. All of our degree programmes are recognised by the Danish Ministry of Education. They are also internationally recognised by the European Credit Transfer System (ECTS).

Academy Profession (AP) degree programmes:

An Academy Profession (AP) degree is an undergraduate academic degree. This comprehensive 2-2½ year programme is similar to the first two years of a Bachelor degree and awards 120 ECTS.

Bachelor's programmes:

A full Bachelor's degree programme lasts for 3½ years. You will be awarded 210 ECTS when you graduate and if you don't want to start working straight away, you can continue your studies in Denmark or abroad to get a Master's degree.

Bachelor's top-up programmes:

Once you have completed your AP degree, you can take another 1½ years of full-time studies and be awarded 90 ECTS. Your AP degree programme combined with the Bachelor's top-up programme is equivalent to a full Bachelor's degree.

Required attachments

You must upload the following documentation with your application:

  • Your upper secondary diploma and transcript of records
  • English test certificate
  • Verification form - signed and stamped by upper secondary school

Optional attachments

If you want to be assessed on criteria in addition to the required attachments, it is your responsibility to upload (along with your application) any documentation for the activities, that you want us to assess. We will consider your activities according to the following criteria:

Other relevant educational experience:

  • Work experience (min. 30 hours per week for min. 16 weeks). Documentation can be an employment contract or an Employer's Declaration (pdf)
  • Activities for a min. 16 consecutive weeks, e.g. Danish folk highschool stays. Documentation can be certificates, contracts, etc.
  • • Voluntary unpaid work for at least 1 year. Documentation can be, for example, a letter of reference.
  • Motivated application (max. 1 A4 page) with reasons for why you decided on your educational programme, knowledge of the academic content of the programme and a career perspective - what do you want to use the educational programme for?

Please note that documentation for the above activities must be in English.

Applicants with an AP Degree from Denmark:

Required attachments:

  • Qualifying AP degree diploma
  • A certificate proving you have at least English B 3,0 (only if you do not have a Danish AP degree taught in English)

In this regard, we must point out, that you must upload your final diploma (a transcript of records is not enough) with your application before the deadline - even though you studied at Business Academy Aarhus before. Only once we have received this document, can we proceed with evaluating your application.

If you apply for admission with study start in January and you complete your qualifying education after the application deadline, you must instead upload a transcript of records listing all of your marks and ECTS credits (without the ones concerning your final exam).

If you are applying with a Danish AP degree, which does not qualify you for direct admission, we will need additional information from you. In this case you must also attach:

  • CV with educational experience and relevant courses
  • Proof of relevant competencies
  • Exemption application (if any)

Applicants without an AP Degree from Denmark:

Required attachments:

  • English test results
  • Upload attachments

Prepare for the future with your internship

As a student at Business Academy Aarhus, you will take part in a compulsory internship where you will be able to use your knowledge in practice. This 2-10 month internship (typically 3-5 months) will give you a chance to experience:

  • Relevant real-life learning in a company
  • Networking and experience that may lead to a future job
  • Working in an international company

Each year Business Academy Aarhus cooperates with more than 800 companies in Denmark and abroad, for example, Carlsberg, Ikea, Nordea Bank, Novo Nordisk and Porsche USA.

Housing for full degree students

You must apply for housing as soon as you have decided to study in Aarhus – before you receive your admission letter.

Business Academy Aarhus is unfortunately not able to offer housing as we do not have any dormitories. However, you can apply for housing via Student Housing Aarhus, which administers residences for students across Aarhus.

How to apply for housing

At studenthousingaarhus.com you will find instructions in English on how to apply for a room, as well as a list of dormitories. These are listed according to town areas and room types (single, couple, couple with children).

You should apply long before you get to Aarhus - the demand is extremely high and the number of places is limited. The waiting times are also usually long, but most students are able to find housing through Student Housing Aarhus if they apply early enough. You can apply up to 6 months before the study start.

Please note that your application has to be renewed each month.

Housing guarantee

All new international students moving to Aarhus for the autumn semester are guaranteed housing.

Other long-term housing

There is also a possibility of renting a room or a flat on your own.

Short-term housing

If your long-term housing is not available when you arrive in Aarhus, there are several options for short-term stays. Please note, however, that these are rather expensive compared to the monthly rent of a student dormitory room.
